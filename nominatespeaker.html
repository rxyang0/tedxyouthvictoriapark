Speaker Nomination Form

TEDxYouth@VictoriaPark is looking for innovators, scientists, students, artists, environmentalists, writers, and any other creative individuals to share their experiences and ideas on our stage. Here, you are welcome to nominate yourself or someone else who you believe may be interested in speaking at our conference.

Our event theme for TEDxYouth@VictoriaPark 2020 is "Ctrl Alt Del". The idea of Ctrl Alt Del is meant to highlight individuals who have dared to take control of their lives by deleting old, fixed-mindset based ideas and pushed their innovative, alternate ideas forward! More information regarding the meaning of this year's event theme can be found here:  http://tiny.cc/ctrlaltdel
